HbA1c (Hemoglobin A1c)
Measures average blood sugar levels over past 2-3 months. Normal range: 4.0-5.6%. Used to diagnose & monitor diabetes.
LDL Cholesterol
"Bad" cholesterol that contributes to plaque buildup in arteries. Optimal: <100 mg/dL. High levels increase heart disease risk.
CRP (C-Reactive Protein)
Marker of inflammation in body. High-sensitivity CRP (hs-CRP) <1 mg/L = low risk, >3 mg/L = high cardiovascular risk.
